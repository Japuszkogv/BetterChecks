⚠️ Requirements: For this plugin to work properly, you must have the following installed: Vault – Required for handling economy transactions. An economy provider (such as EssentialsX, EconomyShopGUI, etc.).

Main Command: /withdraw <amount> Usage: Type the command followed by the number of coins/money you want to take out. Example: /withdraw 500 – This will take 500 from your account and give you a physical paper item (check) worth that amount. How to use the check:

Redeem Money: To put the money back into your account, simply hold the check in your hand and Right-Click it. Trading: You can give the check to another player just like any other item. They can then Right-Click it to claim the money for themselves

📜 PaperCheques – Turn your balance into physical items! Bring a new level of economy to your server! This plugin allows players to convert their virtual currency into a physical piece of paper. It’s the perfect solution for player-to-player trading, event rewards, or safe cash storage. ✨ Key Features: 💰 Physical Currency: Turn your bank balance into an item you can drop, store in chests, or trade. 🖱️ Instant Redeem: Simply Right-Click the cheque while holding it to instantly deposit the money into your account. 🛡️ Secure Trading: No more /pay scams—trade "cash" directly through the trade window or by dropping the item.

🛠️ How it works: Obtain a cheque (via command, trade, or reward). Hold the paper in your hand. Right-Click the air or a block. The paper disappears, and the money is added to your balance!
